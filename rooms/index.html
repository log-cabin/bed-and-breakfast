Mountain Tents

SEASONAL, CALL FOR AVAILABILITY

The larger 12x20 wall tent is used by hunters and outdoor folks who want the feel of camping without the pop up plastic poles and thin material needing an additional cover to stay dry. This tent is made of 15 oz. canvas and can keep warm with the stove inside. The front addition of the tent is the kitchen area with a sink, storage, and a BBQ with sideburner. Just outside is a 6 person picnic table with a fire pit. The main interior of the tent has a queen bed with a wood floor. All the cooking and eating gear is provided. The river is just 20 feet away and a fishing hole is near.
The smaller 'Alaska' tent is a 9x9 with a wood floor 4 person table. It has a pull out futon style mattress. It too has a cook stove and all cooking and eating gear. Also a covered umbrella 4 person picnic table.
Bring a few steaks, drinks, (ice chest is provided), hot dogs and smores. Stay a night or a weekend. We have cut the firewood but bring your own sleeping bag, or we do have them for sale too.
